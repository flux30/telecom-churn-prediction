About This Project

This project addresses customer churn prediction in the Indian telecom sector (Jio, Airtel, BSNL). We implement and compare two supervised learning classifiers to identify customers likely to discontinue service.

The analysis covers customer demographics, usage patterns, service quality, and account history to build accurate predictive models with real-world business applications.
